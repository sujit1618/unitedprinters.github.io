A customer centered approach to print production, perfected since 1999

Established by Raveendra Joshi (R) and Chandrashekhar Joshi (L) in 1999, United Multicolour Printers Private Limited is a leading force in the Indian print industry.

Our area of expertise ranges from pre-press to post-press services, and gives our clients a convenient, one stop destination for all their print requirements. We are constantly developing new possibilities both in printing and in post-print processing through the adoption of new technologies, to give our clients the edge they need in their product quality and delivery speed.

We collaborate closely with our customers to produce high-quality and effective print services. Executing your vision is always our focus. We work with passion on achieving the best solution for you.

Only the best equipment, to consistently provide clients with quality, speed, and cost efficiency.

The first in Pune to own a 5-colour offset machine, the first ones to expand binding and folding set ups, and the first ones to venture in the cloud to improve our processes. We are always innovating and exploring to give our clients the best service and widest range of offerings possible, while keeping their quality high and costs low.

Our service offerings are designed with modularity in mind, giving you the most flexibility in choosing what you would like the final product to be like. This allows us to manufacture all kinds of print jobs, be it high volume, low volume, fully customized, or compliant with industry standards.
