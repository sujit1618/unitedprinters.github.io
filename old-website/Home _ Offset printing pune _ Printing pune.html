GENERAL FAQ
  • General / Company

    What is your phone number and operating hours?

    You can call us at UNITED MULTICOLOR PRINTERS PRIVATE LIMITED at +91 20 24453862 or +91 20 24432280 from 10.00 am to 7pm IST Monday through Saturday. You can send an email at any time.

  • Products / Ordering

    Can I order 2-color printing from you?

    We suggest you get the best value for your money spent is to go for fullcolour, instead of two-color!

    Nonetheless, you may use any of our product pages (with the exception of postcards, business cards, presentation folders, catalogs and calendars) to order two-color printing. Simply place your order as usual, then specify in the comments section that it’s a two-color job and what PMScolours you have selected. Since we are so efficient at producing high-quality four-color work, two-color pieces will be priced the same as four-color pieces. Two-color postcards, business cards, presentation folders, catalogs and calendars will generally be converted to four-color process equivalents before printing. If you need to produce 2-color business cards or postcards using spot inks, you can request a printing price quote online.

  • Mailing Services

    Can you mail my postcards, brochures, etc. if I send you a mailing list?

    Yes! For postcards, brochures and newsletters, United Multicolour Printers Private Limited will be glad to mail your material to a list of mailing addresses that you supply. Please indicate the same well in advance for getting pricing for shipping or mailing on behalf of you and email us your mailing list when placing your order separately.
